THE ONE

CLIENT

Kronos Homes

2019

The "The One" Logo and Visual Identity project centers around the creation of a distinct brand image for an upscale residential development designed by Eduardo Souto de Moura. The project focused on encapsulating the essence of luxury and modernity through a meticulous design process.

From Experimentation to Refinement

The project commenced with an exploration of various design possibilities, each aimed at reflecting the project's status as a luxury apartment complex. The selection of a font with serifs, exuding a sleek and opulent appearance, was a deliberate choice to align with the project's upscale nature. The color palette led to the incorporation of a golden yellow hue, further reinforcing the project's high-end identity.

Elegance in Minimalism

The culmination of the design process resulted in a minimalist logo and visual identity that epitomize sophistication and exclusivity. Through careful consideration of font, color, and overall design elements, the final outcome harmoniously represents "The One" as a beacon of elegance within the realm of luxury residential offerings.
